Research Context

This dashboard was developed during the University of Warwick WMG Summer Research Internship 2026 as part of an applied research project into AI-assisted software engineering practices.

The research investigates the use of AI-assisted software engineering methods for producing cybersecurity education content and cyber range exercises, with a specific focus on exercise design, deployment automation, and evaluation for the CyberRangeCZ platform. The exercises presented below were designed, provisioned, and validated as part of that investigation.
